Shree Swami Vivekanand School (SSV) is a premier educational institution in Vadodara, dedicated to providing a holistic learning experience that nurtures the minds, hearts, and spirits of its students. With a commitment to academic excellence, character development, and community service, SSV empowers young minds to become responsible, compassionate, and successful individuals."

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to foster a dynamic learning environment that inspires academic achievement, personal growth, and social responsibility. We envision a future where our students are confident, compassionate, and equipped to contribute positively to the global community, guided by the principles of truth and integrity.

Core Values That Guide Us

At SSV, our core values include respect, responsibility, integrity, and excellence. We believe in cultivating an atmosphere where every student feels safe, supported, and encouraged to explore their full potential. These values are integrated into every aspect of our school life, from classroom interactions to extracurricular activities.

Dedicated Faculty and Inspiring Leadership

Our strength lies in our highly qualified and dedicated faculty, who are passionate about teaching and committed to student success. Led by visionary leadership, our team works tirelessly to provide a nurturing and challenging educational experience, guiding students towards academic mastery and personal discovery.
